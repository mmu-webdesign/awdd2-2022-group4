FAQ

  • Do I have to show student ID to claim these discount codes in store?
  • Yes, all forms of student ID are acceptable but must be present for you to be able to utilise one of our discounts.
  • Can I submit blog posts to you?
  • Yes, absolutely! Please go to the contact us page and email the team your blog post suggestions. Whether it be topics or full articles we would love to hear from you!
  • Are all the shops you support Manchester based?
  • Yes, we are a brand made in Manchester to represent Manchester and are passionate about supporting the city and the local businesses that make it such a thriving community.
  • How do I become one of the brands you support?
  • Yes, if you’re Manchester based and have some incredible fashion then we’d love to hear from you. Email the team and we will respond back to you in a buzz!
